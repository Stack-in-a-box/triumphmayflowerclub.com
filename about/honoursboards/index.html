Corrections and additions

Information here is open for correction and additions; it has been gathered mainly from copies of Flower Power back to spring 2007 (issue 121) but also thanks to information provided by Peter Burge (20). Given time, older issues of Flower Power will be queried, and a more complete picture will emerge.

If you have any information that can help complete this picture, please send them to our web editor, Rob.

Thanks once again from the members of the club to those prepared to spend their own time making this club run and assisting members worldwide. If you would like to be part of the committee that runs the club, please let the current club secretary know in time for the next AGM.
