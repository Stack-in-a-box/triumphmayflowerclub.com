Upcoming Rallies of Interest to TMC Members in Our 50th Year

Please try to be there if you can!

Rob Davies · 3rd February 2024

This year, being our 50th anniversary as a club, we are trying to make our presence felt at a number of rallies around the country, rather than having one annual rally.

Below are itemised a number of events where a representative (or preferably more than one) of TMC would be desirable. If you go to any of these events, please send some photos, and if possible a few words to Barbara and myself for inclusion in Flower Power / the website.

Rob

In date order (although application forms may need to be in sooner):

Hoveringham Rally

Sunday 26th May

Held at Hoveringham, Nottinghamshire. (It is a very accessible destination NE of Nottingham, just off The Fosse Road (A46) from Leicester and off the A614/A6097 when coming south from the North via the A1.)

Applications need to be made for exhibits by 1st April.

https://www.hvvs.co.uk/ for details and forms.

Triumph Interclub Rally

Friday 28th – Sunday 30th June

Held at the Three Counties Showground near Malvern, Worcestershire. The club has been allocated a pitch for anyone attending.

Applications need to be made for exhibits by 1st April.

Go to https://www.interclubweekend.com/ for details.

Abbey Pumping Station Museum — railway day with classic vehicles

Saturday 7th September

Held near Space Museum, Leicester. Free to attend. Small café on site. Information from John Corley.
