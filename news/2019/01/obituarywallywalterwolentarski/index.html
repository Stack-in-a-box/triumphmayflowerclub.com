Obituary: Wally (Walter) Wolentarski

Written by Russ Hoenig

Russ Hoenig · 12th January 2019

Wally, who was an early member of the Triumph Mayflower Club (№ 93), passed away in the US, where he resided, in November 2018.

Over the years, Wally had met, talked to and corresponded with many members of the TMC, both in the US & the UK; offering parts and suggestions, as well as passing on his experience. His vast collection of Mayflower information has been passed onto the club for all to utilize.

Wally was a good man and a good friend to many people.
