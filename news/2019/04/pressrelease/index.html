Press Release

From the Federation of British Historic Vintage Clubs

Rob Davies · 17th April 2019

Below is a press release from FBHVC with regard to the Ultra-Low Emissions Zone (ULEZ) in London announced recently. In summary, the good news is that classic cars (those which are tax-exempt – including all Mayflowers) do not have to pay the charge, furthermore they do not need to be registered with TFL.

On the negative side, the press release goes on to remind us that our cars are not exempt from the congestion charge – oh well, one out of two isn’t bad!

Rob
