New Spares Available

Wiper arms

Rob Davies and Paul Burgess · 19th December 2018

Paul has managed to find a new supplier who can provide wiper arms with a 15° left-hand crank for the Mayflower at a discounted price (mention TMC). The arms have a stronger coiled spring rather than the blade tensioner but come with ¼″ collets meaning you will have to change them for your original ⅜″ collets.

There are two options:

  1. A stainless-steel version (A90537S) costing around £17.
  2. A polished version (A90537P) coming in at around £19.

Both prices are exclusive of shipping and VAT.

Contact details are: Tex Automotive, +44 (0)1993 89350, info@texautomotive.com.
