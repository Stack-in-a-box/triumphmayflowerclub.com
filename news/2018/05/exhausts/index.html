Exhausts

Supplier of exhausts goes into liquidation – but all is not lost!

Rob Davies · 1st May 2018

Paul Burgess informed me that he noticed in Classic Car Weekly that one of the companies in the suppliers directory, Earlpart (that made exhausts), has gone into liquidation. There was not much information in the CCW article, except that Derby-based Smith Cooper was appointed as liquidator at a meeting on March 16th.

Paul added that he wasn’t aware of anyone who had used them, and that he had only found them from a Google search when researching for the suppliers directory. However, they seemed to be the only company supplying Mayflower exhausts from stock.

He has since informed me that he has found an alternative supplier, PD Gough, who can supply a mild steel system for £290, or a stainless-steel system for £345, both + vat & carriage. See the latest suppliers directory (v8+) for more details.

Thanks for your efforts Paul.
