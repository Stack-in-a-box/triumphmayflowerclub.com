Party in the Park at Husbands Bosworth

Note: Change in date!

Paul Burgess · 22nd June 2018

A quick note from Paul about the ‘Party in the Park’ event in Husbands Bosworth, of which the date has changed from the one listed in the Midlands events calendar:

“Hi everyone,

Driving past Husbands Bosworth this afternoon I spotted an advertising banner for their ‘Party in the Park’ which I was planning to go to tomorrow. The banner said June 30th which is a week tomorrow so I don’t know whether the date has been changed or I got it wrong, but anyway don’t go tomorrow.

Paul”
