Members’ Classifieds

If you would like to place an advert on this page, please get in-touch with our website editor, Rob (contact details in the sidebar), and he’ll get it published.

The advert will remain on the website for three months after the date of publication and will then be removed. If you have success in selling or acquiring your items in the meantime, please let us know so that we can keep the section relevant and up-to-date.

If after three months you wish for your advert to be re-submitted, please contact Rob again; if this is the case, perhaps you could think about altering the asking price, and possibly submit further photos to enhance the listing and boost your chances of attracting a buyer.

If you are not yet a member, feel free to use this facility to advertise all things Mayflower, but a donation of £10 to the club would be appreciated (cheques made payable to TMC, and sent to the editor; address available on request). This will help in our quest to keep these magnificent cars on the road.

Good luck!

Mayflower for sale

“Mayflower 1953, 34k miles. Family ownership for last 25+ years. In good condition, needs a little refresh on seam at top of wings.

Been through a mechanical restoration, which aside from adding electronic ignition (which is inbuilt to the distributor), has been done to original workshop manual specifications.

The following amongst others have been completed:

  • New front coil and leaf rear springs made to spec by Owen Springs.
  • New shock absorbers all round.
  • New stainless exhaust to original pattern.
  • New aluminium fuel tank made to original specification, with safety foam filling by ASD Racing.
  • NOS carburettor supplied by club.
  • New starter motor.
  • New windscreen wiper motor.
  • New full wiring loom by Autosparks to original spec and colours, woven wrap, with additional radio, accessory socket and indicator wiring.
  • Original trafficators still in-situ and can be wired back in as all cables run to switch and chassis.
  • Indicators fitted into matching mayflower rear light pods.
  • New brake master and, where necessary, piping upgraded.
  • New wiper arms and blades.
  • Engine has all gaskets renewed.
  • New radiator rebuilt using original header tank.
  • All new water hoses for heating and cooling.
  • New smiths vintage magnolia gauges fitted (originals provided should you wish to reinstate).
  • NOS manifold fitted, supplied by club.
  • New dash knobs fitted as supplied by club.

Large amount of spares including new dynamo, Nardi electric fuel pump should you need that, etc.

Good seats. Replacement carpet provided as well.

Currently at Mathewsons for the December auction [15th and 16th December – ED].

Would prefer it went to a club member. Can take an offer ahead of auction or bid through the auction. Cost of new parts fitted and work done already all amount to around £5k.

If interested let me know and we can hopefully do a deal to keep it with those that understand the cars.

25% of the net sale proceed will be going to Prostate Cancer UK, as thanks for helping me through surgery and recovery.

Gordon McMurray”
