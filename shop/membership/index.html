Club Membership

Club membership is £30pa with a £5 joining fee. To apply for membership: download, print and fill-in the membership form below along with a cheque, postal order, money order (or set-up a standing order) to the membership secretary, John (contact details in the sidebar). In the future, we hope to make it possible to apply and pay for club membership entirely through the club website.
