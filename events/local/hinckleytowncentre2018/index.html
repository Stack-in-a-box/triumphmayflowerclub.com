Hinckley Town Centre Meet

17th April 2018

Report by Paul Burgess

Whoever usually does the rain dance prior to the Hinckley meets must have been off ill or on holiday today! What a lovely evening, which was reflected in the numbers attending.

John Castle and Chad Brown had met up at John’s at 5:30 PM, Chad bringing his Mayflower out of retirement from Birmingham. Even arriving this early they were still positioned half way up Castle Street Hill.

They managed to save me a space so that we could park up the three Mayflowers together when I arrived at 6:30 PM. John did a good impersonation of a marshal in his high viz jacket. I must get the clutch sorted; it was no fun following the marshal uphill at walking pace with clutch judder.
