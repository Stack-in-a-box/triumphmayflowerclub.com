Events in South Wales and the South West

A list of local events have been compiled into a tabular format for quick reference. The events are not necessarily Mayflower-related but are all to do with cars or transport. All important national events, such as TMC events, are highlighted in yellow for easy identification. Red text indicates events that happened in the previous year and current details of the equivalent events for this year are not yet available.

Click one of the links below for the year you are interested in to open/download the most recent version of that year’s calendar. We will update this PDF as and when new information becomes available and will also indicate the date on which this was updated so that you know whether or not information has changed since you last checked the list.
