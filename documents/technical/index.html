Technical Documentation

This section is an essential reference for anyone working on Mayflowers. It has copies of the official manuals, but also included, and perhaps of more day-to-day use, are the stock lists and alternative part lists provided by Paul Burgess, our spares secretary, together with his compilation of known suppliers of Mayflower parts.

Technical articles featured in Flower Power

Rob Davies

Over the years, Flower Power has featured a number of technical articles – mostly written by club members based on their own experience – pertaining to the maintenance of the Mayflower. The link below will take you to a list of all such articles currently available on the website; the eventual goal is for all articles ever to be published in Flower Power since its inception to be available online in an accessible format, so that it forms a comprehensive handbook containing the club’s collective expertise.

Technical article list

About these documents

Most of the documents included in this section have been provided by Paul Burgess who has sourced and scanned the manuals and documents for our use. Paul has also generated a supplier directory which, whilst not officially endorsed by the TMC, provides a good starting point for members to search for parts and services and, when used in conjunction with the ‘Spare Parts, Codes and Alternatives’ booklet, can make part hunting much easier!
